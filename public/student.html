Student Research at BLE LTER


Our students are based at different institutions affiliated with our projects and come together for field research as well as regular get-togethers and reading seminars.


Sasha Peterson

University of Texas at El Paso

I conduct my research in and around Utqiaġvik located on the North Slope of Alaska. I am interested in better understanding the processes which drive arctic coastal erosion. I plan to explore these drivers through the use of ground penetrating radar, seismicity, and remote sensing. In assessing smaller scale soil properties in relation with larger scale geomorphic features, I hope to recognize patterns of erosion which can be scaled across any arctic coastline. When I am not doing research, I am doing anything outside (climbing, camping, hiking).

Get involved

Interested in keeping up with the BLE student community? Sign up for the BLE student email group. The group serves as the communication channel and forum for reading seminars, professional development opportunities, and other banter.

Email the BLE Information Management team at BLE-IM@utexas.edu to be added to the group.
