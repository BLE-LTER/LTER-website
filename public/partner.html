Partner Projects



Alaska Ocean Observing System

In fall 2020, BLE LTER began a partnership with the Alaska Ocean Observing System (AOOS) as an affiliate in AOOS's next 5-year Intergrated Ocean Observing System plan. This partnership is of key importance in maintaining and expanding our network of moorings across the Beaufort Sea coast. Funding from AOOS goes to instruments recording time series observations of key ecosystems measurements such as pH, temperature, photosynthetically active radiation, and more. See BLE's time series pH data featured on AOOS's website.
